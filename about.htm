Ankit Silicate is manufacturing unit for preparing Sodium Silicates in form of Glass, Liquid, Powder , Sodium Metasilicate Pentahydrate and Sodium Metasilicate Nonahydrate. Over the years, the company has steadily expanded its product range and today we supply chemical raw materials to companies across various industries, Globally.
